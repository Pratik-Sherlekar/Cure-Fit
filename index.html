Joint Pain

If any of your joints hurt follow ICE

I- icing the joint for 20 mins each for 3 times a day. C- compress the joint using any band or even a cloth wrapped around the join for a few hours E- elevate. When sleeping elevate the particular joint by placing a pillow beneath. This will reduce the swelling and allow the wound to heal. Also Immediately stop all physical exercises except the necessities and avoid slopes and weights for 1-2 weeks.

  • Doctor to be consulted: physiotherapist

Overweight

Lets Get Back into Shape

If you are overweight, it restricts your lifestyle and prevents you from living to your full potential. To get back into shape-- Exercise regularly- experts say exercising 4 days a week for 30 mins each is sufficient. Avoid carbohydrates- restrict your carb intake to a minimum for some time Hydrate-Drink plenty of water and juices. Obviously if you need any further Personal Diet, you can always contact our Dietitian for broader nutrition Tips & tricks

  • Doctor to be consulted: Dietitian

A good digestion turneth all to health

Drink warm water with half a lemon squeezed in it. Avoid junk food. Dont lie down and consume things like ginger, mint, cinnamon. Completely omit cigarettes and alcohol. Eat bananas and other binding or easy to digest foods and avoid saturated fats like oil. We are there to help.
